Unleashing the Power of .NET 7

Janvier 01, 2023

In this article, we will be exploring the latest version of the popular software development framework from Microsoft and examining the key features and improvements it brings to the table. We will delve into the performance enhancements made possible by the introduction of EF7 and the ease of migration from .NET 6. We will also take a closer look at the new features of C# 11 and how they simplify development. Additionally, we will share our own experience with .NET 7 and the benefits it brought to one of our clients in the banking industry. This article is intended for developers and IT professionals looking to improve the performance of their enterprise-level applications.
